TuxerOS

TuxerOS is a simple Operating System written in C17 and NASM Assembly. I use the limine bootloader. The current features include:

  • Text Mode
  • Interrupt System
  • A simple Driver Model
And here is what I am planning to implement next:
  • PMM & VMM & Heap
  • FAT32 or Ext2 Filesystem
  • RTC
  • PCI
  • ACPI
  • Serial Interface + USB in general
If you wan't to create your own OS check out Nanobyte's Tutorial. It got me started with OS Development. And I am stilled amazed by how good he is at explaining things. I also recommend the OSDev Wiki for more some other topics That aren't covered in his Tutorial.
